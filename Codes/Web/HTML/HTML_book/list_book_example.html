Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are not my favorite food because I am vegie. Still here is the recipe for that...

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix cream and eggs in a bowl
  3. Once butter is melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan for every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat.
